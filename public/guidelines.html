Community Guidelines – Milan

To keep Milan a safe and respectful space, all users must follow these guidelines:

1. Respect Others

  • Do not harass, abuse, or threaten other users.
  • Respect privacy – do not share others’ personal information without consent.

2. Prohibited Content

  • No obscene, sexually explicit, or pornographic material (Sec. 67, 67A, 67B of IT Act).
  • No hate speech, threats, or illegal content.
  • No spamming, phishing, or scam activities.

3. File & Media Sharing

Files shared must follow our content rules. Illegal or harmful material will result in immediate account suspension.

4. Reporting

If you find any content violating these rules, please report it to our Grievance Officer.

5. Consequences

Violation of these guidelines may result in warnings, suspension, or permanent ban, and may also be reported to law enforcement.
