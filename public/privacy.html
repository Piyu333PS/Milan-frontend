Privacy Policy – Milan

At Milan, we value your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and how we keep it safe.

1. Information We Collect

  • Basic details: username, email, and password (stored securely with encryption).
  • Temporary OTPs for verification (deleted after use).
  • Chat messages are not permanently stored.
  • Files shared are stored temporarily and deleted after transfer.
  • Technical details like IP address and session logs may be collected for security.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide chat and video services.
  • To ensure account security and prevent fraud.
  • To comply with Indian laws and IT Act requirements.

3. Data Security

We use reasonable security measures, including encryption and secure connections (SSL/HTTPS), to protect your data as per Section 43A of the IT Act, 2000.

4. Sharing of Data

We do not sell or rent your personal data. Data may be shared only with legal authorities if required under Indian law.

5. Your Rights

You may request correction or deletion of your account data by contacting us at support@milanlove.in.
